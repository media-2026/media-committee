MEDIA COMMITTEE

Capturing moments, telling stories, and showcasing the spirit of our school through creative media.

Who We Are

The Media Committee captures and shares the life of the school. We document events, create engaging content, and keep students and parents informed through photos, videos, and posts.

What We Do

  • Cover school events such as sports, assemblies, and outreach projects using photos and short videos
  • Manage student-led social media updates, newsletters, and posters
  • Create promotional material for events and campaigns run by other committees
  • Teach media skills including photography, editing, and responsible social media use

Why It Matters

Good media tells our school’s story, celebrates our achievements, and strengthens school spirit. It helps everyone stay connected, informed, and proud of what we accomplish together.
