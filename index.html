MEDIA COMMITTEE CREATIVITY COMPETITION

show us what you can do by participating in one of the following mini competitions in order to be considered for the media committee! dont be afraid to have fun while participating!

Competition Overview

We want to see your creative skills! Winners will be chosen for each category:

  • Best Poster or edit
  • Best Photo / video

Winners will receive a prize (you do not have to win the competition to join the committee this is just a fun bonus reward!

Choose ONE of the following Options thats suits your strenghts

Option 1: Main Character Energy

Pick a main cartoon character from a TV show or movie. Create a poster, photo edit, or video inspired by them or their story.

Requirements:
Poster/ video Edit: A4 size
Video: Max 30 seconds

Option 2: Natural Lighting

Take photos or a short video using natural lighting. Can be landscapes, objects, or anything inspired by nature.

Requirements:
3 photos OR 1 video (max 20 seconds)

How to Submit

Please send your submission through Microsoft Teams. message me (shaylyn Ellen)

Include the following information:

  • Grade & Class
  • Phone Number
  • Category (Poster / Photo Edit / Video)
  • Attach your file(s) according to the chosen option
